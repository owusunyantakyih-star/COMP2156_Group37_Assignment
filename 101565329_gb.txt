George Brown College is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It was established in 1967 and focuses on career-oriented education.
George Brown College has three main campuses: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront. Each campus offers specialized programs in areas such as business, health sciences, hospitality, and technology.
The college emphasizes hands-on learning and practical training. Many programs include co-op placements and internships to prepare students for real-world careers.
