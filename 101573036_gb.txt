George Brown College offers over 170 programs across various fields including business, health sciences, hospitality, and technology.
The college has three main campuses in downtown Toronto: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront.
GBC is known for its strong industry connections, providing students with co-op opportunities and hands-on learning experiences.
The college serves over 28,000 full-time students and 63,000 continuing education students annually.
