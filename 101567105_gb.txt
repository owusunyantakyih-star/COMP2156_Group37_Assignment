
George Brown College is a public college located in Toronto, Canada. It offers career-focused programs in areas such as business, technology, health sciences, and hospitality.
The college emphasizes hands-on learning and practical experience, which helps students prepare for real-world careers. Many programs include co-op placements and internships.
George Brown has multiple campuses, including St. James Campus, Casa Loma Campus, and Waterfront Campus.
The college is known for its diverse student population, including many international students. It provides strong academic support and career services.
